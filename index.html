WILLIAM FOR IT OFFICER

My name is William Hong and I am running for IT officer of the Melbourne Dental Student Society (MDSS) for 2016-17. Learn more here

Why me?

I will be the best person to represent your society as IT officer because of my commitment to serving you, the members, my passion for the job and my hardworking nature.

I have considerable experience that is directly relatable to the position and I will be able to call upon this and apply it in order to faithfully serve you.

IT Experience

The role of IT officer requires the management of the MDSS website. My previous experiences have equipped me with the skills to do this effectively, including:
  • Restructuring and updating the online nomenclature documentation in collaboration with the Human Genome Organisation (HUGO) and the Human Genome Variation Society (HGVS)
    • Sequence Variant Nomenclature
  • Website capable of uploading and recording audio anonymously (application of filters to distort recorded sound) to a database
    • WaveForm

Committee Experience

As part of the MDSS committee, the ability to collaborate and communicate is essential. Dealing with arising issues and making the correct decisions requires experience.

I have faced my share of challenges in my roles as:
